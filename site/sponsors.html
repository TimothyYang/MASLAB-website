Why sponsor MASLAB?

1. Recruiting

Sponsoring MASLAB is an exclusive, face to face, realationship with participants and staff. MASLAB offers an intimate climate where sponsors can learn about and recuit MASLAB students.

With our sponsors, MASLAB can provide an unparalleled educational environment. Your contribution will provide our program with the fuel it needs to continue generating world class professionals for the world.

In addition to a resume booklet for sponsors, MASLAB hosts 3-4 public and/or private functions, providing ample time to communicate with MASLAB participants.
